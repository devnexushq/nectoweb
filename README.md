# 🌐 Necto — The Digital Infrastructure for Local Commerce

> Connecting customers, workers, and local businesses through one unified marketplace platform — starting from Koira, Odisha and expanding across India.

---

## 🚀 About Necto

Necto is a hyperlocal marketplace platform built to help people discover trusted local workers, businesses, products, and services within their communities.

Millions of people in smaller towns still rely on word-of-mouth recommendations to find local services and businesses. At the same time, skilled workers and local shops struggle to build a digital presence and reach new customers.

Necto bridges this gap by creating a simple, accessible, and community-focused marketplace designed specifically for local commerce.

---

## 🎯 The Problem

Local communities face three major challenges:

- Customers struggle to discover trusted local services.
- Skilled workers lack visibility and opportunities.
- Small businesses often have little or no digital presence.

As a result, valuable opportunities remain hidden despite strong local demand.

---

## 💡 The Solution

Necto brings together:

- 👤 Customers
- 🔧 Skilled Workers
- 🏪 Local Businesses

on one platform where they can discover, connect, and grow.

No unnecessary intermediaries. No complicated onboarding. Just local connections made simple.
